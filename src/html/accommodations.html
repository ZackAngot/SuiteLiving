Living Defined by You Accommodations

While living at Suite Living your comfort, safety and security is our utmost concern. We provide a safe and secure setting which includes an electronic call system. We have licensed and certified nursing staff on site 24 hours a day. We also provide housekeeping every week and we have a fully equipped complimentary laundry facility.

Studios & Apartments

Suite Living assisted living offers studios or one bedroom apartments in a central complex with security, housekeeping, laundry, delicious meals and a multitude of social opportunities. The entire complex has been constructed to accommodate and support older adults. Steps have been eliminated, bathrooms adapted, security accentuated and friendly, hoosier staff trained to extend assistance when needed.

Your Retirement Your Way

If you want more independent living we also have spacious two bedroom apartments with attached garages and covered terraces. You can choose to eat your meals in our main dining room overlooking our pond or prepare them in your own fully equipped kitchen.
