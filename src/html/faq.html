Frequently Ask Questions

Q: What is Assisted Living?

A: An assisted living facility offers all the services resident require to remain independent. We provide three meals daily, 24 hour nursing supervision, daily activities, housekeeping and laundry services and social interaction with peers. We are not a nursing home, but can provide services to keep our residents as independent as possible.

Q: What kinds of rooms do you have?

A: At Suite Living we offer two bedroom independent apartments, one bedroom assisted living suites as well as studio suites. Our Independent Living apartments are designed for active seniors who want lots of space but do not want the headaches of home ownership. At Suite Living we mow the grass, shovel the snow and take care of all the home maintenance. In our Assisted Living facility, each suite is equipped with a kitchenette, full bathroom with walk-in shower, and an emergency call system to notify our nursing staff of any needs a resident may have.

Q: Is a large down payment required?

A: Absolutely Not! At Suite Living, each resident signs a lease agreement designed for their specific situation. We do require a $800.00 security deposit which is refundable at the end of the lease period.

Q: Can we rent month to month?

A: Absolutely! Again, we want our residents to have the flexibility they need to feel safe and secure. A month to month option is available as well as a Respite Option which is for residents who want to try out our services before committing to a longer lease period.

Q: What type of food services do you provide?

A: We have structured our meals to include lots of choices. Each resident is offered a weekly menu full of choices. Each meal is designed to offer several choices in entrees, vegetable, and desserts. Our residents are able to choose their menu options based upon their preferences and needs. Suite Living can accomodate residents who are diabetic or who need low sodium diets. Our cooks are always willing to make special meals for our residents upon request.

Q: Can I dine at Suite Living?

A: Absolutely! Meal prices are $6.50 for lunch and dinner. We simply request you call and let us know ahead of time so we can set your place at the table.

Q: Tell me about your activities.

A: We are one of the only assisted living facilities that has a full time activity director who schedules lots of entertainment and fun events. We have weekly bingo and card games as well as board games and special events. Our morning exercise class is very popular with our residents! We schedule weekly bible study as well as church services for our residents. We also go shopping once a week to places like Walmart and local grocery stores as well as visits to the Mall. Once a month we have a special activity day that features fun events and games such as our Beach Party, Valentines Dinner, St Patricks party, April Fools events, Back to School day, Summer Camp, Halloween party....well I think you get the picture.

Q: How much does it cost?

A: The rent we charged is based upon the type of unit and the services required by each resident. Suite Living has the lowest prices of any assisted living faciilty in Grant County. We will be glad to meet with you to discuss the type of unit and services we can offer.

Q: What if we need more care?

A: Our nursing staff can assist with medication setup, reminders, administration. In addition, we can assist with bathing, dressing, and transferring upon request. We are happy to help our residents age comfortably in place!

Q: What do I do with my car?

A: Suite Living is the only assisted living facility to offer car ports to our residents. We have 20 covered parking spaces that are offered at no additional charge.
