Suite Living Who We Are

Suite Living is a locally owned and operated Senior Living Community. The owners have more than 30 years of experience caring for seniors in the Grant County community. Suite Living was built to give local seniors several options that allow for maximum independence and the comfort and services they need to age in place in an environment that offers stimulating activities, scrumptious healthy food, and 24 hour nursing supervision.

There is always fun here Activities

You will never find boredom at Suite Living. When you are not dining on three home cooked meals a day including a full breakfast, you will have plenty of activities to keep you busy. We have indoor activities ranging from bingo and cards to playing the piano. Outdoors, we have periodic family gatherings and barbecues. You are also welcome to explore our walking trail as it meanders on our 21 acres. Of course, you can also try your hand at gardening, riding your bicycle or just visiting friends.

History Opened in 1998

Suite Living was the first free standing assisted living facility to open in Grant County. The owners have a long history of working in the senior care industry and wanted to develop a facility that provided all the daily needs of seniors, and allowed for their maximum independence. Local contractors were used almost exclusively during the construction process and have built a beautiful facility on ten acres of land in Pleasant township of Grant County. The facility has 50 assisted living apartments and 20 independent living apartments. 2018 marks our community's 20th anniversary!
