Services

One of the great features of Suite Living is the additional services that can be obtained for our residents. These include medication administration, additional personal care services such as bathing assistance and daily grooming and dressing assistance, physical therapy, as well as transportation for medical appointments. It is our goal at Suite living to provide these additional services so our residents can remain as independent as possible. We can even arrange to provide Home Health Care through an exclusive agreement with a local home health care agency.

A "Heart Healthy Diet" Dining

Our food service operation is simply the best in Grant County. Our country cooks serve a "Heart Healthy Diet". We are heavy on the meat and potatoes as well as homemade pies (sugar free available) and scrumptous desserts. Suite Living provides our residents with the opportunity to choose several entrees, vegetables, and desserts on a daily basis. Each resident has control over what they would like to be served from our daily menus. We serve three hot meals daily seven days a week, 365 days a year.

Residents are encouraged to invite their friends and family to dine with them. We have a special private dining room that can be reserved for special events such as birthday parties, anniversaries, or other get-togethers. Guests are always invited to dine here at Suite Living. Meal tickets are available for purchase for $6.50.
